Abba's Most Loved

Abba’s Most Loved is an evangelical platform stirred by divine passion to awaken the slumbering hearts of this generation through the truth of God’s Word, fervent intercession, and bold declarations of faith.

We are not a ministry. We are a movement — a gathering of yielded hearts sent by the Spirit to call back the distracted, the weary, and the forgotten into true intimacy with the Father.

Through this platform, we cry loud and spare not, using content that confronts compromise, stirs brokenness, births revival, and exposes deception — all anchored in the fire of the Holy Ghost.

“We don’t gather crowds — we awaken soldiers.”

Welcome to Abba’s Most Loved — where purpose is reignited, truth is unfiltered, and Jesus is glorified.
